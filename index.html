Short Bio

I am a researcher and educator with a Ph.D. in Electronics, specializing in multimedia security, digital watermarking, image/audio/video encryption, and AI-driven content protection. My research focuses on developing robust and intelligent techniques for secure multimedia communication, deepfake detection, and privacy-preserving data handling in smart and healthcare systems. Alongside research, I am passionate about teaching and have experience delivering a wide range of courses at the postgraduate level, including Microcontrollers, Microprocessors, Embedded Systems, Digital Electronics, Signals & Systems, Digital Image Processing, Computer Organization, and Data Structures. I am committed to inspiring students through hands-on learning, strong conceptual foundations, and real-world applications.
